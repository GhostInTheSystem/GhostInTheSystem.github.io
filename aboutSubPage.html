John D. Boswell


Filmmaker, composer, VFX artist, and editor from the Pacific Northwest. His work
spans from television and music production to viral remixes and mashups, and has
drawn over a quarter billion views online

In 2017, MelodySheep created the 8 part series ORIGINS for National Geographic,
and has done extensive work with Disney, PBS, and many other collaborators. A TED
guest and multiple Webby Award winner, he strives to evoke a sense of awe with his
work by celebrating the musicality of life, nature and pop culture.
